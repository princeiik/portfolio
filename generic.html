My Story

A little bit about me...

Growing up, I was that person who would bounce from one interest to another because there were just so many things I wanted to learn and do, but as I've gotten older, programming has been an interest that has stayed with me through the years.

As a software engineer, I come across all kinds of people with different backgrounds and ideas for themselves or their businesses. No matter how big or how small, how complex or how simple, whether I'm with a team or by myself, my goal is to turn their ideas into amazing websites. If I can achieve that and come out with a great product that everyone is happy with, then I've done my job.

When I'm not coding, I'm either playing video games, going on nature walks, or browsing the internet, learning little tidbits about fashion, makeup, space, and the occasional random fact video.
